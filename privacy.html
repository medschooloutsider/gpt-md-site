Privacy Policy

What GPT-MD handles locally, what the merchant handles, and where support data may go.

GPT-MD is a local macOS application. Its export, preview, routing, and diagnostics flow are designed to stay on your machine unless a future release explicitly says otherwise.

This policy mirrors the commercial QA lane and keeps privacy claims aligned with the app's actual behavior.

Data

What GPT-MD uses on your Mac.

GPT-MD may access browser window and tab metadata, page content required for the selected extraction, local app preferences, and diagnostic logs stored on your Mac.

The app is intended to process extraction and export tasks locally on your Mac unless a future release explicitly documents a hosted service.

Browser metadata

Supported Chrome windows and managed Chrome profiles provide the project surface.

Selected content

Only the content required to export the responses you choose should be processed.

Local diagnostics

Diagnostic logs remain on your Mac unless you choose to share them for support.

Diagnostics
