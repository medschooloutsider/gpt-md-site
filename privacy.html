Privacy

Privacy Policy

How GPT-MD handles local app, browser, export, checkout, and support data.

Contact: Email support or open the support site.

Overview

GPT-MD is designed for local macOS Markdown workflows. The app reads supported ChatGPT pages from your local Google Chrome session, allows you to select, rearrange, edit, preview, and export responses, and hands off Markdown output to MarkEdit.

Data processed locally

  • ChatGPT page content visible or available in supported Chrome tabs.
  • Selected ChatGPT responses, response order, and selection state.
  • Markdown preview text and edits made inside GPT-MD.
  • Export output and diagnostic information needed to show errors or troubleshoot extraction/export.

Data the app does not require

Based on the current app design, GPT-MD does not require a GPT-MD cloud account, a Chrome extension, developer-hosted sync, server-side storage of your ChatGPT responses, or Accessibility permission.

Chrome, Automation, and managed Chrome

GPT-MD requires macOS Automation permission to control/read Google Chrome. For the normal Chrome extraction path, Chrome may require Google Chrome -> View -> Developer -> Allow JavaScript from Apple Events. Managed Chrome mode may use a separate Chrome profile and remote debugging; that profile may maintain its own cookies, sessions, and browser state.

Markdown exports

Exported Markdown files may contain any content you selected or edited. You are responsible for storing, sharing, protecting, or deleting exported files. If export opens in MarkEdit, the exported content is handed off to MarkEdit on your Mac.

Payment data

If you buy GPT-MD through a third-party store or payment provider, that provider may process payment-related information such as name, email address, billing information, payment method details, transaction ID, country/region, tax information, refund records, or chargeback records. GPT-MD itself does not need your payment card details to run.

Support emails and diagnostics

If you contact support, we may receive your email address, name if included, screenshots, error messages, diagnostic details, app version, macOS version, Chrome version, and information you voluntarily include. Do not send sensitive ChatGPT content unless necessary for support. Diagnostics are not automatically uploaded to a developer cloud account unless separately implemented and disclosed.

Data sharing and deletion

We do not sell your ChatGPT response content. Limited information may be shared with payment processors, app stores, email providers, or legal authorities where necessary. To request deletion of support-related personal information you sent directly to us, contact Email support. For payment data, contact the relevant store or payment processor. For local exported Markdown files, delete them from your Mac.
